Frequently Asked Questions

Are there headcount minimums? What if we're a smaller company?

There is not! We work with teams of all sizes, and our current clients range in size from 16 - 300+ employees.

How does pricing work?

Our pricing is usage-based, so you only pay for 1:1 sessions with licensed therapists that your team actually uses. You’ll select the number of therapy sessions you want to cover for your team to make sure you can provide high quality care within your budget.
